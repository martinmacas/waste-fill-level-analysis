DETECT_CHANGEPOINT Detects changepoints in time series data.
   [centers, OPERATES, indlast, c] = DETECT_CHANGEPOINT(Time, p)
   uses default optional parameters.

   [centers, OPERATES, indlast, c] = DETECT_CHANGEPOINT(Time, p, 'ParamName', ParamValue, ...)
   allows overriding optional parameters.

   Optional parameters (default values):
       'toler'          - 9 hours
       'timetol'        - 16 hours
       'countappear'    - 5 
       'timeappear'     - 35 hours
       'timedisappear'  - 21 hours
       'nantoler'       - 10 hours
